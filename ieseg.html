  • Established in Lille in 1964, the IESEG School of Management is now one of the top business schools in France.
    IESEG’s mission is to prepare students to become international managers who initiate change, and contribute to the development of responsible, innovative and enlightened organisations.
    IESEG currently hosts 3400 students on both its Lille and Paris campuses, including 1000 international students. More than 84% of IESEG’s faculty is international.

  • Lille

    Lille is a young and friendly city with an animated cultural scene and an outstanding quality of life. It is a unique blend of European traditions, integrating the charm of French city life with Northern European architecture and history. Lille is approximately 50 minutes from Paris.

    Paris

    Paris needs little introduction. The French capital is a major source of history, fashion and food. The city’s rich cultural and historical base, coupled with its tendency to transform and develop, makes it an innovative and dynamic city. Paris was ranked number 1 in the 2014 QS Best Student Cities.

  • French language courses (FLE: French as a Foreign Language) are offered to all exchange students, free of charge.

Living

Accommodation

Housing application details will be sent to students along with their acceptance letter. Housing is allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. It is difficult to find housing on arrival, so students are advised to register for housing as soon as they receive their acceptance from IESEG.
IESEG provides different housing options - student and private residences. Students can choose out of these options, and send their preference via email to the contacts below:
Lille: housinglille@ieseg.fr
Paris: housingparis@ieseg.fr

Health insurance

Non-European students staying more than 3 months must have French health insurance before arrival (around 210 Euros).
IESEG staff will provide students with complementary information and wire payment details. For those staying a full academic year, they must bring a certified birth certificate to go with the registration of French health insurance.

Visa
